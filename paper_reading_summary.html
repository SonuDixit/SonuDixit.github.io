Mathematical Expressions

Here is an example of a LaTeX-based mathematical expression:

Inline: \(E = mc^2\)

Block: \[ \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a) \]

This allows rendering of equations like the Binary Cross-Entropy Loss:

\[ \mathcal{L} = -\sum_{i=1}^{N} \left( y_i \log(\hat{y}_i) + (1-y_i) \log(1-\hat{y}_i) \right) \]
